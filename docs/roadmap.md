## Roadmap

### Phase 1 — Quick Wins
- Field Reports PWA with offline capture and Drive/Sheets integration.
- Opportunity Radar stub with manual refresh.
- Estimate Accelerator MVP and CSV export.
- Smart Contacts JSON loader and search.

### Phase 2 — Automation
- Background jobs to refresh opportunities daily.
- AI summarization of field reports (materials, risks, safety).
- Permissions and OAuth for internal-only access.
- Automated deployments via Netlify and clasp (Apps Script).

### Phase 3 — Acumatica Integration
- Push estimates, opportunities, and customer intel into Acumatica.
- Sync contacts bi-directionally.
- Single sign-on and RBAC.
- Dashboards and KPIs (forecast, labor capacity, pipeline health).
